airjukebox

Air Jukebox is a free Android application which allows anyone to create and take part in social group playlists. Music can be streamed on-demand from the cloud at zero cost, so there's no need to organize your music in advance!

Instantly create a playlist for your event, party or gathering -- just install the application, connect your speakers/audio to your phone, and launch the Air Jukebox app to get started! Anyone with the app at your event will be able to submit song requests, and vote on what's playing next.

We want to create the best way to share music with friends; all we ask in return is that you use it, spread the word, and/or donate to the project if you feel we're doing a good thing!

Geek tip: Air Jukebox is open source software: if you -- or anyone you know -- has the technical skills, you're free to contribute modifications/improvements to it. Our documentation might be of interest for the curious.
